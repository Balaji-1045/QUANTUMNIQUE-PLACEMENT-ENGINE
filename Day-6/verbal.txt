data consistency and performance.

COMMUNICATION PRACTICE

Speak for 3 minutes on:

Explain one challenge you faced while learning coding

Include:
- What was the challenge?
- Why was it difficult?
- How did you overcome it?
- What did you learn from it?





One challenge I faced while learning coding was understanding SQL joins.
At first, I found it confusing to understand how different tables are 
connected. Concepts like INNER JOIN and LEFT JOIN were hard for me to 
visualize, and I often got incorrect results while practicing.It was 
difficult because joins require strong logical understanding, not just
memorizing syntax. Even a small mistake in the condition would give
wrong output.To overcome this, I practiced with simple examples and 
used real-life scenarios like students and marks tables. This helped
me understand how data is combined. With regular practice, I slowly
became more confident.From this, I learned that consistency and 
practice are key in coding, and breaking problems into smaller 
parts makes them easier to solve.
