Question 1
What is the probability of getting a head when tossing a coin?
  Answer:
  = Favorable outcomes / Total outcomes
  = 1 / 2

Question 2
What is the probability of getting a number greater than 4 when rolling a dice?
  Answer:
  Numbers greater than 4 → {5, 6} → 2 outcomes
  Total outcomes → 6
  = 2 / 6
  = 1 / 3

Question 3
A bag contains 3 red balls and 2 blue balls. Find probability of picking a red ball.
  Answer:
  Total balls = 3 + 2 = 5
  Favorable (red) = 3
  = 3 / 5

Question 4
If two coins are tossed, what is probability of getting two heads?
  Answer:
  Possible outcomes → {HH, HT, TH, TT} → 4
  Favorable → {HH} → 1
  = 1 / 4

Question 5
Probability of selecting a vowel from the word ENGINEER.
  Answer:
  Word: ENGINEER → Total letters = 8
  Vowels: E, I, E, E → 4
  = 4 / 8
  = 1 / 2
